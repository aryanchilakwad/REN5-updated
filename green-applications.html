Green Hydrogen Applications

Transforming industries with sustainable hydrogen solutions

Industries Transformed

Transportation Revolution

Green hydrogen is revolutionizing the transportation sector by enabling zero-emission vehicles with fast refueling times and long ranges. Our hydrogen fuel cell solutions power everything from passenger vehicles to heavy-duty trucks and maritime vessels.

  • Zero-emission passenger and commercial vehicles
  • Maritime applications for shipping industry
  • Rail solutions for non-electrified routes
  • Aviation research for sustainable air travel
5 min Average refueling time
500+ km Typical vehicle range

Industrial Transformation

Green hydrogen is rapidly replacing fossil fuels in industrial processes that require high heat or hydrogen as a chemical input. Our solutions enable steel, cement, and chemical manufacturers to drastically reduce their carbon footprint.

  • Direct reduction of iron for green steel
  • Chemical feedstock for ammonia production
  • High-temperature industrial heating
  • Food processing applications
-95% CO₂ reduction in steel production
1500°C Heat capability for industry

Energy Storage Solutions

Hydrogen provides long-duration energy storage capabilities that complement battery technologies. Our systems enable the storage of excess renewable energy for days, weeks, or even seasons, ensuring grid stability and resilience.

  • Seasonal energy storage capabilities
  • Grid balancing and stabilization
  • Renewable energy integration
  • Emergency backup power systems
100+ days Long-term storage potential
80%+ Round-trip efficiency with heat recovery

Residential Solutions

Hydrogen is beginning to enter the residential sector, offering clean alternatives for heating, cooking, and power generation. Our hydrogen blending and pure hydrogen solutions provide pathways to decarbonize homes and communities.

  • Hydrogen boilers for home heating
  • Fuel cell micro-CHP systems
  • Hydrogen blending in natural gas networks
  • Community energy systems
20% Current hydrogen blending potential
90% Efficiency of fuel cell micro-CHP
